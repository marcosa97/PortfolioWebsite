A Little About Me

I am a recent graduate from UC Davis, with a B.S. in Computer Science. While attaining my degree, I applied what I learned inside the classroom to my own personal projects. This is how I got into making games as a hobby to sharpen my programming skills. In order to solve many of the challenging problems game dev presented me with, I learned how to plan and design ahead and the ability to pick up new technologies and frameworks faster.

I am eager to improve my skills by mastering what I know and by learning from others more experienced than me. I am looking for opportunities to work and grow as a developer. Check out some of my projects here and feel free to contact me if you're interested in working together.
